WELCOME to the Belconnen Tennis Club.

We invite you to come along and be part of the fun and fitness of tennis! Only a short drive north of the Canberra CBD, our club offers pennants, mid-week social tennis, junior events and social activities for people of all ages. With a choice of 8 courts, lighting and synthetic grass, there is sure to be a court to suit your game!

New members are always welcome, whether you are new to the game or returning after a break we would love to have you join our fantastic, friendly club. We focus on FUN, FITNESS and FRIENDSHIP at Belconnen.
